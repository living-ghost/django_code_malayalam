Our Humble Beginnings

Founded in 2018 by a group of passionate developers from Kochi, Mallu Coders started as a small meetup group for local programmers to share knowledge and collaborate on projects.

What began as informal coding sessions in coffee shops quickly grew into a movement, as more and more aspiring developers joined our community seeking practical, hands-on learning experiences.

"Our vision was simple - create a place where anyone from Kerala could learn to code, regardless of their background or education."

- Jithin, Founder
